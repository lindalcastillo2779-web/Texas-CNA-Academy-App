Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Texas CNA exam?

The Texas CNA exam is the Nurse Aide Competency Evaluation Program (NNAAP), which tests your knowledge and skills to become a certified nursing assistant in Texas.

How do I register for the exam?

You must complete a state-approved training program and register through Pearson VUE, the testing provider for Texas.

How many CEUs do I need?

Texas requires CNAs to complete 24 hours of continuing education every two years to maintain their certification.

Can I access courses on mobile?

Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.
