About Texas CNA Academy

Texas CNA Academy was founded by licensed nurses and healthcare educators passionate about accessible, high-quality CNA training. Our program combines the flexibility of online learning with hands-on clinical labs to fully prepare you for the Texas CNA competency exam.

We believe every aspiring healthcare professional deserves a clear path into the field, regardless of schedule, location, or learning differences. Our inclusive curriculum is designed for diverse learners including those with disabilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the CNA program take?

Most students complete the program in 4 to 6 weeks depending on their schedule. The online portion is self-paced and the clinical labs are scheduled at local partner facilities.

Is this program approved by the state of Texas?

Yes. Our program is approved by the Texas Health and Human Services Commission (HHSC) and meets all state requirements for CNA certification.

Do I need any prior healthcare experience?

No prior experience is required. Our program is designed to take you from beginner to exam-ready. All foundational concepts are covered in the curriculum.

What does the program cost?

Tuition is affordable and payment plans are available. Contact us at info@texascnaacademyapp.com or visit the enrollment page for current pricing and financial assistance options.
