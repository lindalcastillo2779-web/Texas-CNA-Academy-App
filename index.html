Program Overview

Texas CNA Academy supports every stage of the nurse aide journey, from initial certification through ongoing professional development.

Certification & Exam Prep

Prepare for the Texas CNA certification exam with structured study materials and practice assessments.

  • Practice exams aligned with Texas curriculum
  • Skills checklist and competency tracking
  • Study guides and reference materials
  • Progress tracking and performance analytics

Compliance & Compliance Support

Healthcare facilities and administrators can track compliance, manage staff certifications, and stay compliant with Texas regulations.

  • Staff certification tracking and alerts

  • Staffing compliance dashboards

Ongoing Professional Development

Stay current with continuing education requirements and stay compliant with Texas healthcare regulations.

  • Staff compliance dashboards
