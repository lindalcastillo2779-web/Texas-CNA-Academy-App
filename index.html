FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Texas CNA certification and our platform.

What is the Texas CNA exam (NNAAP)?

The Texas CNA exam is the Nurse Aide Competency Evaluation Program (NNAAP). It consists of a written knowledge test and a clinical skills evaluation. You must pass both portions to become a Certified Nurse Aide in Texas.

How many CEU hours do I need to renew my Texas CNA certification?

Texas requires CNAs to complete 24 hours of in-service education every two years to maintain their certification. Our CEU tracking tool keeps count automatically and alerts you before your renewal deadline.

Is this platform approved by the Texas state board?

Texas CNA Academy is an independent study and tracking platform. Our content is aligned with Texas DADS and HHSC requirements. For official certification, you must register through your training program and Pearson VUE.

Can facility administrators track multiple employees?

Yes. The Staffing Portal allows administrators to manage unlimited staff members, view certification statuses, receive automated expiration alerts, and generate compliance reports for audits.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! You can create a free account and access a sample of our practice questions, one full mock exam, and the CEU tracker. Upgrade anytime for unlimited access to all features.
