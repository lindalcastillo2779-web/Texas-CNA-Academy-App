Why Choose Texas CNA Academy

Everything You Need to Succeed

From first-time students to seasoned CNAs renewing their certification, Texas CNA Academy provides the tools and resources you need at every stage of your career.

Practice Exams

Comprehensive practice tests modeled after the Texas NNAAP exam. Get instant feedback and detailed explanations.

CEU Tracking

Track your continuing education units effortlessly. Never miss a renewal deadline again.

Exam Prep

Study guides, flashcards, and skill checklists aligned with Texas HHS requirements.

CEU Management

Log and track your CEU credits with automated reminders before your renewal date.

Staffing Compliance

Facility administrators can monitor staff certifications and compliance status in real time.

Study Resources

Video lessons, downloadable guides, and interactive quizzes designed for busy healthcare workers.

About Texas CNA Academy

Built for Texas Healthcare Professionals

Texas CNA Academy was created by healthcare professionals who understand the challenges of nursing education and career advancement. Our platform is designed to make certification, renewal, and professional development accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

To empower every Texas nurse aide with the knowledge, resources, and support they need to excel in their profession and provide the best possible patient care.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing accurate, up-to-date, and accessible content that meets Texas HHS requirements and supports the highest standards of nursing care.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Texas CNA Academy free?

Our basic exam prep and practice questions are free. Premium features like CEU tracking and advanced analytics are available with a subscription.

Is the content HHS-aligned?

Yes, all our content is aligned with Texas Health and Human Services (HHS) requirements for CNA certification and renewal.

Can I track CEU credits?

Yes, our CEU tracking feature lets you log credits, upload certificates, and receive automated reminders before your renewal deadline.

Can facilities manage staff compliance?

Yes, facility administrators can monitor staff certifications, track CEU compliance, and receive alerts for expiring credentials.

Is it mobile-friendly?

Yes, Texas CNA Academy is fully responsive and works on all devices, so you can study and manage your credentials on the go.
